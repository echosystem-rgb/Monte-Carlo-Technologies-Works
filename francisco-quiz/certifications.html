International Safety Standards

EKO MOTO products are certified to meet strict international safety standards, including:

  • CE Level 2 (CE EN 13595-1): Full protection for racing and track use. Impact-absorbing panels on shoulders, elbows, and hips.
  • CE Level 1 (CE EN 13595-2): Urban protection for street riding. All critical impact zones protected.
  • EN 1621 Armor Certification: All padding and armor inserts meet European armor standards.
  • Material Standards: Leather tested for abrasion resistance and strength. All materials sourced and verified for durability.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Every piece of EKO MOTO gear undergoes rigorous testing before it reaches you:

  • Material abrasion testing on specialized equipment
  • Impact testing of all armor components
  • Seam strength and durability testing
  • Real-world crash testing and evaluation
  • Professional rider feedback and iteration
