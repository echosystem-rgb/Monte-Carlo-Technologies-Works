Our Story

The Beginning: 2001

EKO MOTO was born out of frustration. In a cramped garage in Southeast Asia, a group of track day riders were tired of gear that didn't deliver real protection. Commercial options were either cheap imports that fell apart on first contact with asphalt, or European imports that cost more than the bike itself.

So they did what riders do—they started building their own. Hand-cutting leather panels, testing designs on the track, iterating with every crash and close call. The first jackets were crude by today's standards, but they held. They protected. They worked.

Growing Pains: 2005-2010

Word spread through the region's track community. Riders noticed the quality. They talked to friends. The garage operation expanded to a small workshop, then a proper facility. We started working with professional race teams, incorporating their feedback into every new design.

These early years taught us the value of obsession. We tested gear in ways manufacturers rarely do—real crashes, real speeds, real consequences. Every design flaw was a lesson. Every piece of feedback from a professional racer was gospel.

Global Expansion: 2010-2020

By 2010, EKO MOTO had cracked the code. Our gear was being used by professional racers across Asia and the Pacific. We expanded to Europe, then North America. We achieved CE Level 2 certification, proving our designs met the most rigorous international safety standards.

Innovation became our hallmark. We pioneered new material combinations—aerospace-grade Cordura blended with kangaroo leather, Kevlar reinforcements in high-impact zones, titanium sliders on our competition models. Each innovation came from real-world testing, not boardroom decisions.

Today: Uncompromising

Today, EKO MOTO is trusted by professionals and enthusiasts worldwide. We've equipped 47,000+ riders. We've tested gear on 34+ racing circuits. We've partnered with 100+ professional teams. But we haven't lost sight of our roots.

Every piece of gear that leaves our facility is built with the same obsession that started in that garage in 2001. We're not just making jackets and pants—we're building protection. We're honoring the riders who trust us with their safety.

Our Promise

We promise to never compromise on protection. We promise to stay connected to our community of riders. We promise to keep innovating, keep testing, keep pushing the boundaries of what protective gear can be. Because at EKO MOTO, we're not in business to sell gear. We're in business to keep riders safe so they can do what they love: ride.
