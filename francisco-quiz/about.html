About EKO MOTO

From a Garage to the Grid

EKO MOTO started in 2001 in a small motorcycle repair shop, where riders began hand-cutting leather panels for their own track gear because nothing on the market could survive a real crash.

Today, we're trusted by professional racers, track day enthusiasts, and street riders worldwide. Every piece is engineered to protect, tested on the circuit, and built to last through the toughest conditions.
