Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

EKO MOTO collects information to process orders, improve our services, and communicate with you. We collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, shipping address, and billing information
  • Payment Information: Payment method details (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Browsing Data: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device type for security and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send shipping notifications and order updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure payment processing, and restricted access to personal data. However, no online transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to protecting your information.

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Trusted payment processors
  • Shipping and logistics partners (to deliver your orders)
  • Law enforcement (when legally required)

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to store your preferences, shopping cart, and login information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, data requests, or concerns, contact us at:

Email: privacy@ekomoto.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our website constitutes acceptance.
